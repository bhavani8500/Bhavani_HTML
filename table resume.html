Professional Summary

A professional summary, also known as a career summary or executive summary, is a brief statement at the beginning of your resume or CV that provides a concise overview of your qualifications, skills, and career objectives. It is typically used to grab the attention of employers and provide them with a quick snapshot of your suitability for a job. A well-crafted professional summary can help you make a strong first impression and increase your chances of getting noticed by potential employers.
